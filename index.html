About JF Air and Heat

BUILT ON EXPERIENCE, DRIVEN BY TRUST

Owner Jason Ferraccio has been in the HVAC industry since 1987. Originally from Buffalo, NY, Jason moved to Charlotte in 1998 and quickly became a field supervisor at one of the city's largest HVAC companies.

In 2015, Jason launched JF Air and Heat to deliver the kind of service he believed every homeowner deserves -- honest, affordable, and fast. Today, he and his team serve Charlotte and the surrounding 50-mile radius with the same hands-on commitment.

Jason lives in South Charlotte with his wife and two children. When he's not on a service call, you might find him with his two pugs. Your comfort is his goal -- superior service without the high cost.
